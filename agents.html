Intelligent Oloren Agents

Oloren Agents revolutionize organizational operations by harnessing the power of intelligent automation. As digital entities, Oloren Agents seamlessly collaborate with human teams, leverage the capabilities of Oloren Orchestrator, and offer a streamlined process for their creation and deployment. Oloren Agents enable rapid productization of your workflows.

Agents as Employees

Oloren Agents act as highly capable digital employees in a specific domain, driving automation and efficiency in your organization. These entities use your workflows and integrations to intelligently automate tasks. Oloren Agents can handle entire workflows, freeing up valuable human resources.

Agents and Orchestrator Superpowers

The combination of Oloren Agents with Oloren Orchestrator unlocks remarkable capabilities. Agents seamlessly integrate with Orchestrator's extensive toolkit, accessing workflows, functions, and extensions. This powerful synergy enables agents to perform complex tasks, handle intricate workflows, and interact with various systems and data sources. The result is agents super connected to your ecosystem. Instead of agents that just telling you what to do, Oloren Agents simply can do it for you.

Streamlined Agent Creation

Creating Oloren Agents is a straightforward process. With Oloren Nexus, anyone can design and export workflows as agents without extensive coding knowledge. The Oloren Nexus Agent Builder utilizes advanced AI language models and an adaptable interface, making it easy to deploy agents. Oloren Agents leverage the network computation capabilities of Oloren Orchestrator to be super functional and the generative capabilities of Oloren InterfacePro to dynamically create custom UI. Once created, these agents can be easily integrated into existing workflows or sold as standalone products.
