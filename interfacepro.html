Introducing InterfacePro: Simplify Custom UI Creation and Secure Data Usage

InterfacePro offers a comprehensive AI solution for custom user interface (UI) creation with robust data security. With InterfacePro, streamline UI development by leveraging intuitive tools and seamless backend integration capabilities.

Effortlessly Design Custom UIs

InterfacePro simplifies UI design by combining advanced AI language models and Oloren's CodeCraft React package. By utilizing InterfacePro as an AI frontend assistant, designing custom UIs in Orchestrator becomes effortless. You don't need any React expertise or extensive coding knowledge - simply use InterfacePro.

Unlock the Power of Orchestrator Integrations

InterfacePro’s UI creation process takes advantage of Orchestrator’s capabilities to reduce integration complexity and increase functionality. With Orchestrator and InterfacePro, your UIs become programmable, shareable, and extensible.

Data Security at the Core

We place the utmost importance on data security. Oloren will always offer a self-hosted/on-premises deployment solution, allowing you to maintain complete control over your data and infrastructure. Your data remains confidential and can never be accessed by anyone.

Elevate Your UI Development with InterfacePro

InterfacePro simplifies UI creation, streamlines backend integration, and ensures data privacy. Whether you're developing enterprise applications, customer portals, or internal dashboards, InterfacePro provides a powerful framework for building custom, super functional UIs that meet your unique needs.
