Orchestrator System

Graph Editor

A visual interface for building workflows. Begin by loading up Oloren's suite of extensions, and then easily drag, drop, and connect nodes to compose your computational workflow. If you're a developer, you can build your own custom extensions and add them to the editor. Otherwise, no code required!

CodeCraft

The magic glue. Oloren CodeCraft converts your existing monolithic scripts into an Oloren compatible extension, unlocking all the functionality of Oloren's Orchestration system. Functionalize your existing code, compose your script with other nodes, and build complex is applications.

Nexus

Oloren Nexus is Oloren's productivity hub for teams. With Nexus, host workflows on the cloud, integrate with other extensions via the store, and use Oloren InterfacePro easily export your team as an API, intuituve UI template, or LLM-powered agent. Regardless of how computationally focused your team is, Nexus leverages Oloren's IntefacePro make workflows more functional.

Third Party Extensions

Beyond Oloren's Basics extensions for Google APIs, OpenAI Text Completion APIs, PDF parsing, data manipulation, JSON capabilities, and more, we also support third-party extension development. Anyone can develop and offer extensions with Oloren Nexus. Please reach out if you are interested in creating or codeveloping new extensions for Oloren AI. We're partnered with some domain experts to build Orchestrator Extensions, and would love to work with more collaborators.
